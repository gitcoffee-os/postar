## 🌟 Key Features
### 📝 Content-Focused
   - Supports articles, notes, moments, images, videos, audio and more.
   - Text content supports HTML and Markdown formats.
###  [🌍 Multi-Platform](https://postar.exmay.com/docs/media)
   - Covers international mainstream media platforms including X (Twitter), Facebook, Instagram, TikTok, YouTube, LinkedIn, Medium, Dev.to and more. Easily extensible to support WeChat, Weibo, Xiaohongshu, Zhihu, Bilibili and other domestic platforms.
### 🚀 One-Click Publishing
   - Edit and format once, sync and auto-publish to multiple platforms with one click.
   - Simultaneously publish content to all logged-in media platforms.
### 🔐 Secure
   - The core differentiator from other tools is the local operation mechanism — directly reusing the browser's local login state, avoiding the privacy risks of storing account information in the cloud. Safe and worry-free.
### 📖 Smart Reader
   - Built-in smart web reader for intelligent content extraction from web pages.
### 🎯 Platform Adaptation
   - Automatically optimizes content adaptation to meet the specific requirements of each platform.
### ✨ AI
   - Extensible AI integration for more personalized content and smarter workflows.
### [🧩 Extensions](https://postar.exmay.com/docs/extension)
   - Through plugins, SDKs, APIs, MCP, AI agents, open platforms and app marketplaces — extensible and connectable to various industry platforms or enterprise application services.
### 💡 Ecosystem
   - Provides ecosystem support for content creator productivity and developer business integration.
